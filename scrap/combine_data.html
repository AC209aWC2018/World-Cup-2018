Merging Sofifa Datasets¶

As our data collection from sofifa resulted in several datasets, we merge them all together before preparing them for our training and test datasets.

ratings = pd.read_csv('data/team_stats.csv')
ratings_ext = pd.read_csv('data/team_stats_ext.csv')
ratings_players = pd.read_csv('data/team_stats_players.csv')

# convert date for ratings
ratings['date'] = pd.to_datetime(ratings['date'], utc=True)
ratings_ext['date'] = pd.to_datetime(ratings_ext['date'], utc=True)
ratings_players['date'] = pd.to_datetime(ratings_players['date'], utc=True)
